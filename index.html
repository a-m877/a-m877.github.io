AIRN lncRNA Data

AIRN is a long non-coding RNA, which is conserved in both humans and mice. In humans, two transcripts are produced from the gene - an unspliced variant and a spliced variant.

In mice, there are eight AIRN transcripts, which are all spliced.

Airn overlaps the second intron of the IGFR2 gene, on the antisense strand. Mutation in Airn is associated with Wilms tumour and susceptibilty to glioma.
